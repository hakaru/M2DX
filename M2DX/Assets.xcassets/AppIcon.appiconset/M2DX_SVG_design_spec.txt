
M2DX SVG Logo Design Specification
=================================

Purpose
-------
This document defines how to recreate the M2DX logo as a *pure vector SVG*,
suitable for UI, print, and scalable rendering.

Canvas
------
- ViewBox: 0 0 1000 300
- Background: Transparent
- Alignment: Horizontal center

Typography
----------
- Base style: Custom geometric sans-serif
- Stroke: None
- Corners: Slightly rounded (radius ≈ 4% of stroke width)

Letter Construction
-------------------
M:
- Wide stance, symmetrical
- Vertical stems slightly tapered inward

2:
- Continuous curve
- Flat horizontal base
- Thickness matches M stem

D:
- Half-oval outer curve
- Inner counter scaled to 70%

X:
- Two diagonal strokes
- Overlap at exact center
- Angle ≈ 32 degrees

Color
-----
Primary Gradient (Left → Right):
- #E6E6E6 → #00C8D7 → #0077C8

Flat Variant:
- Monochrome white (#FFFFFF)
- Monochrome black (#000000)

Export Rules
------------
- No raster effects
- No blur, glow, or shadows
- All paths expanded
- Stroke converted to outline

Recommended Files
-----------------
- M2DX_logo.svg
- M2DX_logo_flat_white.svg
- M2DX_logo_flat_black.svg
